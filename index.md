Infinite Defense – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 21, 2025

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Infinite Defense (“we”, “our”, “the App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, even though this App collects minimal personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information
The App does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

1.2 Advertising Information
The App integrates Google AdMob for serving ads. Google AdMob may collect the following information:

Advertising ID (AD_ID)

Device information such as model, OS version, app version, and network type

Coarse location inferred from IP address

Usage statistics related to ads

This information is collected only for ad serving, ad measurement, and fraud prevention. All data is handled according to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
.

1.3 Usage Data
Anonymous usage data and crash logs may be collected only by Google AdMob to improve ad delivery and performance. No personal data is collected or stored by us.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected by Google AdMob may be used for:

Displaying personalized or non-personalized ads

Measuring and analyzing ad performance

Preventing ad fraud

Improving the quality and relevance of ads

We do not access or store this information directly.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses only Google AdMob.

No Firebase, no analytics SDKs, no Facebook SDK, no third-party plugins are included.

All data collected by AdMob is governed by Google’s privacy policy.

4. Permissions

To comply with Android 13+ (API level 33) requirements, the App requests only the following permissions:

Advertising ID Permission

<uses-permission android:name="com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID"/>


Used by Google AdMob to access the device’s Advertising ID for ad delivery and measurement.

Internet Permission

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.INTERNET"/>


Required to load ads and connect to the ad network.

No other permissions are requested.

5. Data Retention and Security

The App does not store or retain any personal data.

Data collected by Google AdMob is stored and processed according to Google’s security policies.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years old (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that your child has provided personal data, please contact us at the email below, and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

Even though the App collects minimal personal data, users have the following rights:

Reset your device’s Advertising ID or disable personalized ads via system settings

Access, correct, or request deletion of any information stored by Google AdMob

Uninstall the App to stop data collection

Contact us regarding any privacy concerns

For users in the European Union (GDPR):

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

You may also withdraw consent for personalized ads at any time.

For users in California, USA (CCPA):

You have the right to request disclosure of the categories of personal information collected by the App.

You may also request deletion of personal information collected by the App.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in laws, services, or App functionality.
The latest version will always be accessible via the official App store listing or website.
